COACHING   

Splatoon 3 

What to do:

Spread apart from your team in turf wars - Spreading apart will allow your team to each ink parts of the map
Occasionally flanking the enemies team in ranked games- Since it's not about having the most turf, you can sneak behind the team and try to kill some of them
Saying booyah when getting 2 kills or more- Saying Boo-yah after getting 2 or more kills is just a fun thing to do; There's nothing special about it
Using different kinds of weapons- Using different kinds of weapons will give you more knowledge on what weapons to use instead of your main weapon you use
Use the new tactics in the game- The new moves in splatoon 3 are very versatile and can be used for many different things. It can easily turn a 1v1 around for the better
Play Salmon Run- Salmon Run is a great online co-operative game mode to play for when you want to play as a team with other people instead going against them. It follows you and 3 other people to gather special eggs from the enemies called salmonoids. You go through 3 waves with each one getting harder and harder. There are special waves that can happen which can be based around 1 boss and there's only about 4 of these special waves. There's also a new final wave which is a huge boss and its name is the Cohozuna and the most efficient way to kill it is to throw eggs you gather from the regular bosses and throw it at it to deal large amounts of damage.

What not to do:

Do not spam the communication button- Spamming the communication buttons is very annoying to your teammates unless they decide to do it with you
Not helping your teammates out- Whenever a teammate or teammates calls for help by saying "This way", they usually need help with pushing into the enemies team territory
Not participating in Splatfest- Splat-fest is a huge event that usually happens on the weekends and it's all about helping and rooting for your team. You will also gain rewards for being in splat-fest and winning it

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

What is it:

Splatoon 3 is an online multiplayer, third-person shooter kind of game and is owned by Nintendo. The main story follows a squid (inkling) in a deserted area where you can make your own character and you get on a train to a new city called splatsville. In that place you can start your splatoon experience. You are told that the main power source of the city was stolen and this is where you get the option to play the story mode. If you don't want the story then you can find the online game in the lobby. The main focus of the game is to the most turf.

Splatoon 3 has a rating of 83% on open-critic, meta-critic and a 9/10 on Nintendo life and IGN. This game is for ages of 10 or up. This page is for newbies to mid-level gamers and this can be found helpful to them because it explains the basics of the game and a few tricks and tips.

There are tutorials to learn how to play the game and the only way to get better is to keep playing the game and asking Judd the cat for tips and tricks. There are no specific strategies to play the game and the game is somewhat easy to pick up due to how straight forward the game is.

There aren't that many mechanics to Splatoon 3, but all you have to mostly do is mostly work with your team, communicate, and be versatile with weapons and your play-style because people around the world can easily learn how you play.
